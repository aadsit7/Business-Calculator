How these numbers are calculated

Capacity = packaging automation + reuse + ticket deflection, all in engineer hours:

  • Packaging: unique packages/yr × hours each × effort removed by automation.
  • Reuse: duplicate per-customer rebuilds/yr × hours each × the share reuse eliminates.
  • Tickets: addressable app tickets/yr × deflection rate × minutes to handle each.
  • More customers = freed hours ÷ your current app-delivery hours per customer.

Security window compares your current urgent-update propagation time with Application Workspace's near-immediate delivery. Onboarding, consistency, advanced delivery and Intune extension are shown as outcomes, not added to the dollar figure, to keep the capacity number conservative and defensible.

Benchmark defaults are grounded in Recast's published material — the Setup Store's daily-updated catalog with CVE context, ~90% reduction in app configuration/deployment/update effort, and agent-based installs completing in minutes versus Intune's hours-long pending states. Sources: Application Workspace for Intune, Setup Store Connector, Deploy using Intune.
